ACTIVITY DIAGRAM PER FITUR SIMASJID
===================================

1. ACTIVITY DIAGRAM LAPORAN KAS
-------------------------------
```plantuml
@startuml
skinparam defaultFontSize 16
skinparam backgroundColor white
skinparam defaultFontName Arial
skinparam ActivityBackgroundColor white
skinparam ActivityBorderColor black

|Admin|
start
:Akses Menu Kas;

|System|
:Tampilkan Data Kas;

|Admin|
:Pilih Aksi CRUD;
:Input/Edit Data;

|System|
:Validasi & Simpan;
:Update Database;
stop

@enduml
```
